About Me

My name is Alessandro Felix and I am from Peru. I love spending my free time snowboarding whenever I get the chance to hit the mountains. I enjoy learning new things, staying active, and exploring new places whenever I can.

Lima, Peru

Lima is the capital and largest city of Peru, located on the country's arid Pacific coast. It is home to a rich mix of colonial architecture, modern skyscrapers, and some of the best cuisine in the world. Lima is often called the "Gastronomic Capital of the Americas" thanks to its world-renowned restaurants and diverse culinary traditions.

My Favorite Hobby

Snowboarding is my favorite hobby. There's nothing quite like carving down a fresh slope with the mountain air rushing past. It keeps me active, clears my mind, and gives me something to look forward to every season. Whenever I get the chance, I try to hit the mountains and catch a few runs.
